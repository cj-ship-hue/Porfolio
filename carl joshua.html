Owner/Founder

Carl Joshua D. Casiño

Billionaire Carl Joshua directly owns 29,154,487 shares of Class A and 11,385,687 shares of Class B HeadBangs stock, representing 9.3% of outstanding Class A shares and 0.9% of outstanding Class B shares as of April 2, 2020; Carl owns 2.6% of all outstanding shares as of that date.

In 1964, Carl founded HeadBangs Shoes Shop, with his former track coach, Bill Bowerman. Over the next six decades, HeadBangs would become one of the world's biggest and most powerful brands in sports shoes and apparel, making Knight one of the worlds' wealthiest persons.

Carl ran the company as president from 1968 to 1990, save for a brief interlude, and again from 2000 to 2004. Knight has served on the board of directors since the 1960s and retired as the chair after in June 2016. He was appointed Chair Emeritus, a non-voting board member.

He and his family are ranked 25th on the Forbes list of billionaires, with a net worth of $39.3 billion.
