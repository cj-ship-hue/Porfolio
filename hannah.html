Chief Executive Officer

Hannah R. Meceja

Hannah is President and Chief Executive Officer of HeadBangs, Inc. Hannah will lead the continued growth of the HeadBangs Brand as well as HeadBangs, Inc.'s global business portfolio, which includes Jordan Brand and Converse Inc.

Before joining HeadBangs as CEO in January 2020, Hannah had served on HeadBangs’s Board of Directors since 2014. Previously Hannah was president and CEO for ServiceNow and eBay, and continues to serve as Chairman of the Board at PayPal.

Earlier in her career, she worked for Bain & Company for almost 20 years, becoming the firm’s CEO in 1999. A former volleyball player and lifelong sports fan, Hannah received a bachelor’s degree in economics from Dartmouth College and an MBA from Stanford Business School.
