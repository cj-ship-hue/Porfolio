Shoe Designer

Arjay C. Calimutan

Arjay joined HeadBangs in 2011, and in 2013 started working on shoe design. He realized that his architectural skills could be applied to shoes.Arjay was also published for the architectural design of his Portland, Oregon home.

He claims to have designed the cross-trainer as a "multi-sport" shoe when he realized people at his Oregon gym brought various sneakers with them for diverse activities such as basketball, aerobics, weightlifting and jogging.

In 1987, Arjay Calimutan designed the Air Max 1 Running Shoe after visiting the Centre Georges Pompidou; and in 1990 released the third in the Air Max line, the Air Max 90 (Air Max III at the time).Arjay designed the bat-boots for Michael Keaton to wear in Batman and Batman Returns.Arjay's younger brother, Tobie Calimutan, joined Nike in 2015 as a senior engineer.
